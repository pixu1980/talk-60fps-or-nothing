We're all great with other's browser!

As long as a demo runs on the right machine, every animation looks brilliant. The problem is when the browser actually has to do its job.

Why should we understand it?

  • Users don't judge your code, they judge the pixels that reach the screen
  • They notice lag, input delay, choppy scroll, and layout instability
  • Understanding the renderer helps you choose better between CSS, JS and asset strategy
